Our Story

Born from a love of the craft, and a belief that great coffee changes your morning.

Ember & Ground started as a small roastery tucked in a Pune lane in 2018. We believed then — and still do — that the best cup of coffee comes from knowing exactly where your beans come from, how they were grown, and who picked them.

Today we source from three family farms across Coorg, Araku, and Chikmagalur, roasting in weekly small batches to keep flavour at its freshest.
